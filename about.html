About St. Michael the Archangel Parish

Traditional Roman Catholic St. Michael the Archangel Parish is a Traditional Roman Catholic parish dedicated to preserving the perennial teachings, sacred traditions, and sacramental life of the Holy Catholic Church. Guided by fidelity to Sacred Scripture, Sacred Tradition, and the traditional Roman Rite, our mission is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, administer the Holy Sacraments, and provide spiritual care to all who seek God's mercy and grace. Our parish is a ministry of The Society of St. Alphonsus Maria de Liguori, also known as the Alphonsian Redemptorists, whose apostolate is inspired by the zeal of St. Alphonsus Maria de Liguori for the salvation of souls and the proclamation of the Catholic Faith. Our Ministry The parish is home to the Office of Deliverance, Liberation, Healing and Exorcism, Inc., a ministry devoted to assisting the faithful through prayer, pastoral guidance, spiritual discernment, and the sacramental life of the Church. Our ministry seeks to lead souls closer to Jesus Christ through repentance, conversion, healing, and deeper communion with God. We encourage the faithful to embrace a life of prayer, frequent reception of the Sacraments, devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary, and confidence in the powerful intercession of St. Michael the Archangel, defender of the Church and protector against the powers of evil. Our Patron St. Michael the Archangel is honored as the heavenly protector of our parish. As the Prince of the Heavenly Host, he stands as a symbol of courage, fidelity, and unwavering obedience to God. His victorious proclamation, "Quis ut Deus?" ("Who is like God?"), reminds all Christians that no one is equal to the Almighty. Our Spiritual Foundation Inspired by the words of Sacred Scripture: "Quia apud Dominum misericordia, et copiosa apud eum redemptio." "For with the Lord there is mercy, and with Him is plentiful redemption." — Psalm 129 (130):7 our apostolate strives to proclaim God's infinite mercy and His abundant redemption to all who seek Him. We likewise profess the ancient Catholic maxim: Extra Ecclesiam Nulla Salus "Outside the Church there is no salvation." affirming our commitment to the fullness of the Catholic Faith and the mission entrusted by Christ to His Holy Church. Our Mission Our mission is to: Preserve Traditional Roman Catholic Faith. Lead souls to Jesus Christ through the Sacred Liturgy and the Holy Sacraments. Provide spiritual guidance through the ministries of deliverance, liberation, healing, and exorcism in accordance with the Church's spiritual tradition. Foster holiness through prayer, devotion, catechesis, and Christian charity. Defend the faith and encourage all people to live according to the teachings of Christ and His Church.
